Welcome to Job Manager!

Do you want to find a job of your dreams in IT? Maybe you've already got a work, but you need some new challenges? Or simply, current earnings are insufficient? We have prepared something special for You!

We would like to present Job Manager. An app which will help you find dream job. App is divided in three tabs. First named „Offers” contains a list of offers.It is possible to filter them! Go there and see how easily you can find an intresting offer!

When you find the right ad, add them to your favorites and follow them in the tab „Favs”.

We left the best in the end! In the tab „Stats” you can compare salaries from different destinations and different technologies!

We wish you a successful search!
